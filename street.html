Street Shots

Raw, bare, stark, rustic, simple, straightforward and yet the streets hide so much more, mysterious in their own ways, brimming with tales of present of the moment. Streets for me are a hub of picture stories, the treasure to find hidden gems and lost moments.

Streets are not the place for words, but for the emotions, the feelings, the moment, the vision, the frame captured. Streets tell the stories of the cities untold. They are untouched, pure and full of zeal, full of enthusiasm in hardships, full of sunshine in the dark. The streets are my absolute favourite for solo photowalks.
