Travel Story

Travel rejuvenates the soul. It adds life to the lifeless of days, creates so much to look forward to. Each city, its people, its places, its culture, its food, its festivals make a complete different volume of millions of stories. These stories can forever be preserved in pictures. These stories are the life of this place.

Heritage forms a major part of my interest in Travel photography. Each building thousands of years old, each stone, each marble speaks for itself, each window looking out into a world of their own. These pictures are the identity of the city miles away from the city. These experiences fill travellers with the fire to know more, the desire to explore more. To find the undiscovered, to capture the ordinary and make it grand.
